Top 5 Wireless Earbuds Under $50

October 10, 2023 By Audio Expert Earbuds

Gone are the days when good wireless audio required a hefty investment. Today's budget earbuds offer impressive sound quality, decent battery life, and useful features that rival more expensive options. After testing 15 different models, here are our top 5 picks under $50.

Our Testing Methodology

We evaluated each pair of earbuds based on these criteria:

  • Sound Quality: Clarity, bass response, and overall balance
  • Comfort & Fit: How well they stay in during various activities
  • Battery Life: Actual vs claimed battery performance
  • Features: Touch controls, app support, water resistance
  • Build Quality: Durability and charging case design
  • Value: Overall package for the price

The Top 5 Budget Earbuds

1. SoundCore Life P2i - $39.99

Best Overall: Excellent sound quality with good bass, 7-hour battery life, IPX7 waterproof rating. Perfect balance of features and price.

Pros: Great sound, good battery, very waterproof

Cons: Case is a bit bulky

2. JLab Go Air Pop - $24.99

Best Value: Surprisingly good sound for the price, 8+ colors available, 32+ hours total battery with case. Unbeatable at this price point.

Pros: Super affordable, fun colors, good battery

Cons: Basic charging case, no app support

3. TOZO T6 - $35.99

Best for Water Resistance: IPX8 waterproof (can be submerged), wireless charging case, good touch controls. Ideal for workouts and outdoor use.

Pros: Excellent waterproofing, wireless charging

Cons: Sound could be more balanced

4. EarFun Free 2S - $45.99

Best Sound Quality: Surprisingly detailed audio with good bass, Qi wireless charging, IPX7 waterproof. Best sound in this price range.

Pros: Excellent audio, wireless charging

Cons: Slightly above $50 on some days

5. Skullcandy Dime 2 - $29.99

Most Compact: Tiny case that fits anywhere, Tile finding technology, good for small ears. Perfect for portability and occasional use.

Pros: Very compact, Tile integration, affordable

Cons: Shorter battery life (3 hours per charge)

Key Features to Look For

When shopping for budget wireless earbuds, prioritize these features:

Feature Why It Matters Minimum to Look For
Battery Life How long you can use them 5+ hours per charge
Water Resistance Protection from sweat and rain IPX4 (splash proof)
Charging Case Portability and extra charges 2+ additional charges
Controls Ease of use Basic play/pause controls
Fit Options Comfort and security Multiple ear tip sizes

Common Budget Earbud Pitfalls

Avoid these common issues with cheap earbuds:

  • Terrible Microphones: Many budget options have poor call quality
  • Unreliable Connectivity: Look for Bluetooth 5.0 or higher
  • Cheap Build Quality: Plastic that cracks easily
  • No Warranty: Reputable brands offer at least 1-year warranty
  • Fake Battery Claims: Real-world battery is often less than advertised

Our Testing Verdict

After extensive testing, we found that the budget earbud market has improved dramatically. You can now get 80% of the performance of premium earbuds for 20% of the price. The key is to manage expectations and choose based on your specific needs:

  • For sound quality: EarFun Free 2S
  • For value: JLab Go Air Pop
  • For workouts: TOZO T6
  • For portability: Skullcandy Dime 2
  • For all-around use: SoundCore Life P2i
