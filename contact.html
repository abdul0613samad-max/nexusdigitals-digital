Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping across UAE for orders above AED 100. For orders below AED 100, a flat shipping fee of AED 15 applies. Delivery typically takes 2-3 business days within Dubai and 3-5 business days for other emirates.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to GCC countries including Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, and Bahrain. International shipping rates vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy for defective products. Products must be in original packaging with all accessories. Return shipping is free for defective items. For change of mind returns, customers are responsible for return shipping.

Do your products come with warranty?

Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranty ranging from 6 months to 2 years depending on the product. Warranty information is provided with each product and can be claimed through our support team.
